How It Works

Three Steps to a
Self-Maintaining Tank

The Smart Auto Top-Up Water system is brilliantly simple.

1
Sensor Detects Drop
The precision float sensor continuously monitors your water level. The instant it drops below your set point, the controller detects it and activates.
2
Pump Activates Silently
The quiet built-in pump draws water from your 20L can or bucket and delivers it smoothly into your tank via flexible tubing — no splashing, no mess.
3
Perfect Level Restored
Once the correct level is reached, the system stops automatically. Your aquarium stays perfectly topped-up every hour of every day.
